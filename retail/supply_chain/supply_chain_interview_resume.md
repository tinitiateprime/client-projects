## 🎯 Interview Q&A
**Q1. Why build a supply chain data platform?**  
**A:** To get end-to-end visibility from purchase order to final delivery and reduce delays/costs.

**Q2. What are key supply chain KPIs?**  
**A:** On-time delivery, fill rate, lead time, damage rate, and receiving accuracy.

**Q3. Why separate shipment and receiving tables?**  
**A:** Shipment tracks planned/transit flow; receiving confirms what was physically accepted.

**Q4. How do you manage partial deliveries?**  
**A:** Record partial receiving events and keep PO line balances open until completion.

**Q5. How do you detect delayed shipments?**  
**A:** Compare ETA with delivered timestamp and flag SLA breaches.
